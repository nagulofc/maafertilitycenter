Our Services

Range of services offered by Maa Fertility Centre

With our unmatched facility and advanced Technology, Maa Fertility is well equipped for a futuristic approach towards reproductive health. As the best Fertility Centre in Bangalore, we offer treatments and services discussed below

Fertility Enhancing Endoscopic Surgeries

Laparoscopy and Hysteroscopy (instrument introduced through vagina and cervix to visualize the womb) are the commonly performed endoscopic surgeries in infertility management.

Male infertility evaluation & treatment Andrology Lab

Our andrology laboratory offers several tests that can help your treatment process, including, anti-sperm antibody test and semen analysis.

Intrauterine Insemination (IUI)

Intrauterine insemination (IUI) is a method that puts washed and processed sperm straight into the uterus assisting healthy sperm to move closer to the egg.

In Vitro Fertilization (IVF)
Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection (ICSI)

IVF and ICSI are forms of assisted reproductive treatment (ART) in which eggs are combined and fertilized with sperms, outside the body.

Frozen Embryo Transfer (FET)

FET cycle is thawing one or more embryos from a previous fresh IVF and transferring these to the uterus to establish pregnancy.

Surgical Sperm Retrieval (PESA/TESA)

PESA and TESA are the methods that can be used to extract sperm, if a man does not have sperm in his semen (azoospermia); has low sperm count, abnormal sperms; poor motility sperms.

Hormone Analysis

Hormonal evaluation studies identify hormonal imbalances that may hinder your fertility. We evaluate and determine the best treatment options.

Laser-Assisted Hatching

Laser-helped incubating is a trend-setting technology utilized alongside IVF treatment to build the rate of successful implantation of the embryo.

Preimplantation Genetic Diagnosis
Preimplantation Genetic Screening

PGD identifies genetic abnormalities in preimplantation embryos prior to embryo transfer, so only euploid embryos established from IVF are transferred.

Preimplantation Genetic Diagnosis
Preimplantation Genetic Screening

PGS is a screening test that can be performed on embryos created via in vitro fertilization IVF to genetically analyse the embryos prior to transfer.
